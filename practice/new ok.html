USA Visa Process

Determine the Type of visa

Description: Identify the visa that fits your purpose of travel. Common types include:

  • F-1: For students.
  • B-1/B-2: For business or tourism.
  • H-1B: For specialized workers
  • J-1: For exchange visitors.
  • L-1: For intra-company transferees.

Pay the Visa Fee

Description: Pay the non-refundable visa application fee online or at designated banks Fees vary depending on the visa type.

Complete the DS-160 Form

Description: This is an online application form for non-immigrant visas.
Key Point: Save the DS-160 confirmation number for your visa appointment.

  • Visit the Consular Electronic Application Center (CEAC) website
  • Provide personal, travel, and security-related information
  • Upload a passport-sized photograph as per guidelines

Schedule the Appointments

Description: Book two appointments via the US Visa Application Center (VAC):
Key Point: Ensure the appointments are at least one day apart.

  • Biometric Appointment: For fingerprints and a photo.
  • Visa Interview Appointment: At the US Embassy or Consulate.

Prepare for the Visa Interview

Description: Gather and Organize required documents
Key Point: Practice answering common interview questions confidently.

  • Passport (valid for at least six months beyond your intended day)
  • DS-160 confirmation page
  • Visa fee payment receipt
  • i-20 form (for students) or other supporting documents

Attend the Visa Interview

Key Point: Approval may be given on the spot, or you may need to wait for further proccessing.

  • Be honest and provide clear answers
  • Submit additional documents if requested
